About Me

Hello, my name is Nicolas Joukowski, I was born in Volos, Greece and I'm a graduate from SAE Athens Greece with a bachelor's degree in Games Programming. What inspired me to become a game developer is my love for video games, that I have been playing since I was four years old. When I was seventeen, I decided to get a deeper knowledge and education about my passion, game development. What I absolutely love about this industry is level designing, because it unleashes my creativity and boosts my productivity. Since then, I have participated in several projects and "game jams" that really taught me to cooperate harmonically with my team and work hard to achieve a common goal.
